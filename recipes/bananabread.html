Banana Bread

Description

  • 3 Bananas
  • 1 Cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Scoop of Protein Powder (24g)
  • 3 Egg Whites
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Cup of Ground Oats

Steps

  1. Smash bananas to a mushy texture
  2. Grind Minute Oats in blender
  3. Mix all ingredients together
  4. Pour mixed ingredients into oven safe dish
  5. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  6. Bake mixed ingredients for 30 minutes!

This is a low-calorie and high protein custom Banana Bread!

100 calories / 10 grams of protein per serving!
